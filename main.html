About Us

  • IEEE SB LBSITW is one of the most active Student Branches of IEEE Kerala Section. We as a team encourage divergent thinking in students by providing various opportunities.
  • IEEE SB LBSITW was established on 4th of August, 2012.
  • IEEE has several societies that offer various benefits. There are currently 3 societies (CS, RAS & SSCS) and 1 affinity group (WIE) active in our SB.
  • We conduct several events from intra-collegiate to National levels both independently and in coordination with several active bodies of IEEE.
