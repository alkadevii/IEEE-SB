About Us

IEEE SB LBSITW, established on August 4, 2012, is one of the most active student branches under IEEE Kerala Section. It promotes technical growth, innovation, and leadership. With active societies like CS, RAS, SSCS, and WIE, our SB empowers students through meaningful events.
